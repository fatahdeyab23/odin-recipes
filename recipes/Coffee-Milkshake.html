Coffee Milkshake

You might just want this coffee milkshake for those mornings when you struggle with the antiquated practice of losing an hour each springâ€”the jolt of caffeine sure wakes me up!

Ingredients

  • 1 shot espresso
  • 4 ounces milk
  • 3 tablespoons malted milk powder
  • 1 tablespoons dark chocalte syrup
  • 2 table spoons whipped cream

Steps

  1. Pour cooled espresso shot and milk into a high-powered blender, such as a Vitamix.
  2. Add the malted milk powder and ice cream.
  3. Secure lid; blend on High until smooth, about 45 seconds.
  4. Drizzle syrup on the inside of a glass.
  5. Pour milkshake into glass. Add whipped topping.
