Watermelon with Feta

This chunky watermelon salad with feta, arugula, red onion, and tomatoes is enjoyed by all.

Thanks to the watermelon, the kids like it also!

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons white balsamic vineger
  • 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 8 ounces grapes tomatoes, halved
  • 3 cups arugla
  • 1 cup sliced red onion
  • 2 pounds watermelon, cubed
  • 4 ounces feta cheese, cut into 1/4-inch cubes

Steps

  1. Whisk olive oil, vinegar, and salt together in a large mixing bowl.
  2. Add tomatoes, arugula, and red onion; toss to coat.
  3. Gently stir in watermelon and feta cheese to serve.
